Story behind the music...

Black Sheep tells the story of DRE and the many struggles he has faced throughout his life. Like most going through difficult times, he felt that he was experiencing these struggles alone, feeling completely outcasted. In time, he realized many deal with these same emotions, and he realized these experiences and range of emotions are common.
